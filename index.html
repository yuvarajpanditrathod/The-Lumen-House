What Goes Wrong

Common Lighting Issues

Visual Fatigue & Glare

Too many lights lead to visual fatigue. Poor placement causes glare resulting in discomfort and headaches.

Imbalance & Mood

Uneven lighting creates an unsettling imbalance. Incorrect color temperature negatively impacts environmental mood.

Inefficiency & Waste

Poor angles cast unwanted shadows. Wrong illumination levels result in highly inefficient spaces and wasted energy.
